Eigenvalues & Variance

Eigenvalues ($\lambda$) dictate the "length" of the principal components. The larger the eigenvalue, the more variance that component explains.

Notice how stretching the data into a line pushes almost all the variance into PC1, allowing us to drop PC2 entirely with minimal information loss.
